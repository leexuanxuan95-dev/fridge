Fridge

Snap a grocery receipt. Your fridge tracks itself. Quiet alerts 2 days before food expires. Stop tossing $1,500 of food a year.

Download on the App Store

How it works

📸
Scan a receipt
Whole Foods, Walmart, Costco, Trader Joe's, Kroger — each has a hand-tuned parser. Anything else falls back to a smart generic parser.
⏰
Storage-aware expiration
Chicken lasts 2 days in the fridge, 9 months in the freezer. Move an item between locations and Fridge re-estimates.
🔔
One quiet reminder, not ten
Same-day expirations are aggregated. A daily 9 AM digest gives you the week ahead.
🛒
Smart shopping list
Finish a carton of milk — it shows up on your next shopping list. Share via Messages with one tap.
📊
Monthly insights
See what you saved and what you tossed. Top-wasted items help you fix the habit.
👨‍👩‍👧
iCloud Family share (Pro+)
Everyone in the household sees the same fridge in real time, end-to-end encrypted by Apple.

Private by default

No accounts. No tracking. Receipt photos and food data stay on your device. We don't have a server, and we don't want one.

Get it

Fridge is iPhone-only, iOS 17 and above. Download on the App Store (link goes live on launch day).
